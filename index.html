About Our School

St. Francis School is committed to academic excellence and holistic child development. With modern facilities, dedicated teachers, and strong values, we prepare students to excel in academics, creativity, sports, and life.

St.Francis School,Vadakkencherry is an English Medium co-educational school conducted by the Franciscan Fathers and Brothers who are members of the Third Order Regular (T.O.R.) of St.Francis of Assisi, after whom the school is named. Our Education Ministry is to spread the love of God in the hearts of all people, in the spirit of St. Francis of Assisi, by imparting the values of human dignity, universal brotherhood, equality and justice.
